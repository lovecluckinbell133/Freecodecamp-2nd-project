John Ronald Reuel Tolkien

One of the most well known fantasy writers in history

A short summary of Tolkiens life:
  • John Ronald Reuel Tolkien was born on 3 January 1892 in Bloemfontein in the Orange Free State (later annexed by the British Empire; now Free State Province in the Republic of South Africa)
  • He initially studied classics but changed his course in 1913 to English language and literature, graduating in 1915 with first-class honours
  • Some time in the early 1930s Tolkien started writing The Hobbit, he initially wrote the story to entertain himself and his children, but in the year 1936 a company with the name Allen & Unwin heard about it and wanted to publish it. The 21st of september 1937 the book was published and sold unexpectedly good.
  • He died 2 September 1973 (aged 81) Bournemouth, Hampshire, England J.R.R Tolkiens wikipedia article
